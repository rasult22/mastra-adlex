## GENERAL TERMS & CONDITIONS:

## 1. IFZA has the right to cancel or change a business name if an existing trade name has been found to be similar to another name.

## 2. IFZA has the right to cancel or change a business name if it is not meeting any of the above guidelines.

## 3. The guidelines do not apply to companies with already reserved trade names.

## 4. If the customer failed to issue the commercial license, the trade name is automatically cancelled at the end of the reservation period.

## Reservation period is up to three months.

## 5. These guidelines are valid and they are in place starting from 1 November 2023. These are subject to change as per the rules and

## regulations of UAE.
